About

I build stuff—systems, interfaces, and little digital ecosystems.

I code, design, and animate interactions, and I enjoy thinking about how all the pieces fit together so that everything just works. I like exploring both the big picture and the tiny details—how a system behaves, how an interface flows, and how motion can make something feel alive.

I experiment across development, UI/UX, and motion to create experiences that feel smooth, intuitive, and intentional. Mostly, I just like building things from start to finish, connecting different skills, and seeing how far I can push an idea while keeping it usable and enjoyable. If something seems simple on the surface, I like to know exactly what makes it tick—and make it tick better.

What I build

Web apps, native tools, browser-based systems, document platforms, IDE concepts, automation dashboards, and embedded IoT projects.

How I work

I move across code, UI, UX, and interaction design to shape products that are both technically solid and pleasant to use.

What I care about

Code quality, clean UI/UX, strong systems thinking, and making ambitious ideas stay practical, understandable, and reliable.

Process

How I approach building.

01 — Explore

Understand how it works

I like getting beneath the surface of a product or system and understanding what makes it tick.

02 — Connect

Join disciplines together

Code, UI, UX, and motion are treated as connected parts of one experience rather than separate tasks.

03 — Build

Ship practical systems

Whether it’s software, hardware, or both, the goal is to build something that actually works and stays usable.

04 — Refine

Polish the experience

I keep pushing details until the interaction feels smoother, clearer, and more intentional without losing utility.
